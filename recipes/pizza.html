Pizza

Description

A classic homemade pizza recipe that's easy to make and full of flavor. Customize your toppings to create your perfect slice.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onions
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) and place a pizza stone or an upside-down baking sheet in the oven to heat.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the rolled-out dough to a pizza peel or another floured surface that's easy to transfer from.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle half of the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Layer on the sliced pepperoni, olives, bell peppers, and red onions.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella cheese over the toppings.
  8. Season with dried oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  9. Carefully slide the pizza onto the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet in the oven.
  10. Bake for about 10-12 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  11. Remove from the oven and drizzle with olive oil.
  12. Allow the pizza to cool slightly before slicing and serving.
