Omelette

Description

A classic omelette is a quick and versatile dish that's perfect for breakfast, brunch, or even a light dinner. Made by whisking eggs and cooking them with various fillings, omelettes are a delicious and customizable option for any meal of the day.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter or cooking oil
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1/4 cup diced bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced ham or cooked bacon
  • 1/4 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Chopped fresh herbs (such as parsley or chives) for garnish

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl. Add a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper.
  2. Use a fork or whisk to beat the eggs until the yolks and whites are well combined and slightly frothy.
  3. In a non-stick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of butter or oil over medium heat.
  4. Add the diced onion and bell pepper to the skillet. Saut√© for 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and add the remaining butter or oil to the empty side.
  6. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, making sure they spread evenly.
  7. Allow the eggs to cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to set.
  8. Once the edges are set, use a spatula to gently lift and push the cooked eggs towards the center, allowing the uncooked eggs to flow to the edges.
  9. Sprinkle the diced ham or bacon over one half of the omelette.
  10. Sprinkle the shredded cheddar cheese over the same half.
  11. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  12. Cook for an additional 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked to your desired level of doneness.
  13. Slide the omelette onto a plate and garnish with chopped fresh herbs.
  14. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious omelette!
