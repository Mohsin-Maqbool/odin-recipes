Classic Garden Salad

Description

A refreshing and nutritious classic garden salad filled with fresh vegetables and a zesty vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce mix
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Red onion
  • Carrot
  • Bell pepper
  • Olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Croutons

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce mix.
  2. Peel and slice the cucumber.
  3. Dice the tomato and bell pepper.
  4. Thinly slice the red onion.
  5. Grate the carrot.
  6. Combine all the vegetables in a large bowl.
  7. Add olives, feta cheese, and croutons.
  8. In a separate bowl, mix together your favorite vinaigrette dressing.
  9. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  10. Serve immediately and enjoy!
